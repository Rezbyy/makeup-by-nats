Meet Your Artist

Where every look tells a story

With over a decade in bridal makeup, Nats has worked with hundreds of brides across New Zealand and beyond, bringing calm, expertise, and a meticulous eye to every single booking.

Specialising in both Western and cultural bridal makeup, Nats creates everything from soft, romantic looks to bold, photographically stunning applications. Whether you're planning an intimate ceremony or a grand multi-day celebration, she delivers looks that last and makes every client feel extraordinary from the first touch to the final dance.
