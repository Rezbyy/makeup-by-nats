Service 02

Cultural
Weddings

Deeply rooted in her own Sri Lankan heritage, Nats understands the beauty and significance of cultural wedding traditions. She approaches every cultural ceremony with genuine respect, sensitivity, and a thorough understanding of the aesthetics that matter most to you and your family.

Whether it's a South Asian celebration, a multi-cultural ceremony, or any cultural tradition in between, Nats has the experience and cultural fluency to create looks that are both authentic and stunning. She can also accommodate two looks for brides celebrating across multiple ceremonies.

What's Included
  • In-depth pre-wedding consultation for all events
  • Skin prep guide
  • Long-wearing makeup application
  • Dupatta / Kandyan dressing & jewellery placement
  • Touch-up kit provided
  • Custom lashes
  • Bridal trial recommended
  • Travel available for NZ & destination weddings
  • Cultural bridal makeup & hair packages available

Service 04

Education &
Masterclasses

With over a decade of professional experience, Nats is passionate about giving back to the makeup community. Her masterclasses and 1:1 education sessions are designed for aspiring and working artists who want to elevate their craft.
