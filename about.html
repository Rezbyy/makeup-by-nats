Who I Am

A passion for beauty, a gift for people

My journey into makeup artistry began long before it became my career. Growing up surrounded by a rich Sri Lankan heritage, colour, beauty, and self-expression were always central to how I understood the world around me.

In 2015, a friend's referral to a production agency changed everything; it was the spark that lit a fire I've never been able to put out. From that very first professional application, I was hooked. I invested in 1:1 lessons and masterclasses with some of the industry's best, sharpening my craft until I was ready to build something of my own.

In 2019, I made the move to Aotearoa New Zealand, and it was here that my career truly blossomed. The warmth and diversity of this country resonated deeply with me, and the incredible brides I've had the privilege of working with have shaped me into the artist I am today.

I believe makeup should never overshadow; it should illuminate. My approach is always about enhancing what's already there, making you feel like the most confident and radiant version of yourself.
